About Sky Oak Realty™

We are a legacy real estate company in Houston, Texas with over 30 years of sales and service to our friends and neighbors. We specialize in first time home buyers and those relocating to this great city from throughout the U.S. and abroad. Let us help you to find your dream in Houston, Texas or in one of its unique and hospitable neighboring communities.

A Great American City

America's International Boomtown

Houston is not just an oil town but a thriving global hub. The most international city in America which people from all over the world call home. You can call this amazing city your home as well. From the pines of The Woodlands, to the bars and shops of Montrose, to the shinning new communities of Pearland, across to Katy and Richmond/Rosenburg, all the way to the beautiful shores of Jamaica Beach, we will not only help you find a home but introduce you to all that America's 4th largest city has to offer.
